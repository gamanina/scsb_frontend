外籍人士辦卡專區
Application criteria 申請條件

The SCSB credit card application criteria are as follows:

Primary cardholders’ age should be between 20 and 75. Supplementary cardholders must be primary cardholder’s parents, parents in-laws, spouse, or children and sibling with age of 15 and not above 75. Applicant under 20 shall obtain consent of his/her guardian.


Type of Card Minimum annual income required
Classic NT$220,000 and above
Gold NT$300,000 and above
Eternity/ Precious/Platinum NT$400,000 and above
Signature Business/ Titanium NT$600,000 and above
World Business NT$2,000,000 and above

The required application documents are as follows:

For foreigners:

  • Completed application form
  • A Taiwanese guarantor is required
  • Photocopies of the Alien Resident Certificate (ARC, both front and back) and passport
  • Applicant’s and guarantor’s financial supporting documents：last income tax bill or six months’ paychecks or other relevant documents (i.e. bank’s term deposit or copy of house tax bill)

SCSB reserves the right to request for financial supporting documents or other relevant documents and to grant final approval


Report of loss or stolen 遺失處理

Please call us immediately to report the lost or stolen card.

  • Local：0800-003-111
  • Oversea：886-2-2558-1311

After reporting the lost or stolen card, we will issue a new card as replacement for you.
Handling Fee for Report of Loss or stolen is NT$200.（World Business card is free；One card is NT$100）


Revolving Credit Interest 循環信用利率

The calculation of revolving credit interest shall be from the date of entry of the account to be calculated into the revolving credit interest, based on an annual interest rate of 5.46%~15.00% until the full payment of the said account.

Debit card 金融簽帳卡

The Debit Card is not a credit card. It is a card with a general ATM and debit function that can be used for domestic and overseas ATM and purchase transactions. The transaction amount will be deducted directly from the cardholder’s TWD deposit account. The Debit Card does not have the function of delayed payment; neither does it have the functions of cash advance or overdraft. No annual fee for the Debit Card.

The SCSB debit card application criteria are as follows:
Primary cardholders’ age should be 15 and above, who shall hold SCSB deposit account. After opening deposit account, cardholders receive debit card immediately
