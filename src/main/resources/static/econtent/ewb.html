Q&A
Q1. Is it secure to use eWB internet banking for money transfer?

Our e-World Banking system adopt Financial Extensible Markup Language certificate which is used in data transfer or processing in internet and is verified by W3C company in 1997. The standard is emphasizing on data structure description without complicate configuration process and easier to get verification in seconds. To achieve the goal of transferring money among different banks with one certificate by using international standard, it is easier to get the conformity and accordance when exchanging data among banks.

The standard also provides solution of cash flow management solution and integration of electronic business with one certification.

The certificate is verified by Taiwan CA Inc. which is an international trust third party. The certificate is well known by its security and widely adapted to endure data transmission of the confidentiality, security, integrity, recognition and undeniable transactions in the domain of banking transfer and confirmation of identification by encryption and decryption of electronic signature.

Q2：What kind of software version for operation system and browser?

We suggest you use the following versions：

  • Operation system : Windows 7 or above
  • Browser: windows Internet Explorer 11
Q3：Should all the transferred bank account names and numbers be appointed before using the eWB service?

To protect your right of depositor, whenever you revised the bank account number and money transfer limitation you have to our any branch to complete the application form with Identification card. For revising inward remittance bank account number of NTD and foreign currency, you can complete the process on the internet by yourself.

For revising bank account number of foreign currency inward remittance, you can revise on internet by yourself or go to our any branch to apply. After our approval of internal process, you can use it.

Q4：Does the eWB provide 24 hours service?

eWB is 24 hours service whole year except financing functions which is available from 9:00 am to 18:00 pm for bank's internal processes. In case of system maintenance, the system will be closed temporarily.

Q5：Does eWB provide function of letter of credit issuing? Any other export bills functions?

eWB provides to issue letter of credit and it can save your cost of delivering original application form of credit issuing. You can use "sample management" to have your own files to improve your efficiency by avoiding data key in. eWB will inform beneficiary by e-mail when finishing approval of issuing processes. You can also monitor progress and record of application by enquiry functions.

eWB also provides functions of credit amendment, Open Account Financing, export bills negotiation, export and import bills collections.

Q6：Does eWB have the functions of financing and refunding?

Yes, for your cash management, you can apply financing credit limit which approved by our bank at your account branch, then you can have the functions of e-financing and e-refunding.
